Welcome to Health Home!

Health Home will soon be your ultimate fitness companion! Health Home offers a range of features, including:

  • Calculating your BMI and tracking it over time
  • Workout and diet logging
  • Messaging functionality (for trainers and athletes)
  • Goal setting and monitoring (for athletes by trainers)

Health Home is designed to help you achieve your fitness goals in collaboration with your trainer or as part of your personal journey. Whether you are a gym-goer, an athlete, or a trainer with knowledge to pass on, sign up today!
